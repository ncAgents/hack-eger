Choose Your Track

Each track represents a different area of innovation and technology. Pick the one that aligns with your interests and skills, or explore something completely new. You'll work with mentors and fellow participants who share your passion for that particular domain.

AI & Machine Learning

Participants will explore intelligent systems, build data-driven applications, and experiment with cutting-edge AI technologies.

Blockchain & Web3

This track focuses on decentralized applications, smart contracts, and innovative use cases for financial inclusion and transparency.

Sustainability & AgriTech

Hackers will develop solutions addressing food security, climate challenges, and sustainable farming practices.

Open Innovation

A flexible track for any groundbreaking idea that doesn't fit into the others, encouraging creativity and cross-domain innovation.
