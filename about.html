About Hack Egerton

Empowering Students to Code the Future

Our Mission

Hack Egerton is a student driven hackathon by EUCOSSA that empowers students to solve real world challenges through technology. From AI and Web3 to sustainability and automation, the hackathon provides a platform for creativity, collaboration, and innovation helping students turn ideas into impactful solutions that shape campus life and beyond.

What We Do

Innovation

We foster creative thinking and innovative solutions to real-world problems on campus.

Automation

We build systems that automate repetitive tasks and streamline campus operations.

Community

We bring together students, mentors, and industry professionals to collaborate.
