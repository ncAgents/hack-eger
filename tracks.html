Hackathon Tracks

Choose your focus area and build something amazing

Choose Your Track

Each track represents a different area of innovation and technology. Pick the one that aligns with your interests and skills, or explore something completely new. You'll work with mentors and fellow participants who share your passion for that particular domain.

AI & Automation

Build intelligent systems that automate processes and improve daily life, including campus automation projects.

Web3 & Blockchain

Create decentralized applications, smart contracts, and blockchain tools for transparency and financial inclusion.

Smart Agriculture & Food Systems

Apply data, IoT, and decision support tools to improve farming, livestock, and food distribution.

Financial Automation & FinTech

Develop digital payment solutions, budgeting apps, or fintech systems for accessible financial services.

Education & Learning Tech (EdTech)

Build platforms, AI tutors, or collaborative tools that enhance teaching and learning experiences.

HealthTech & Wellbeing

Innovate around mental health, telemedicine, or clinic resource management to support healthier communities.

Sustainability & Environment

Create solutions for renewable energy, waste management, and environmental conservation.

Engineering & Hardware Innovation

Work on robotics, embedded systems, IoT devices, and engineering driven physical prototypes.

Data Science & Analytics

Turn raw data into insights, dashboards, and predictive models for informed decision-making.

Open Innovation (Wildcard)

Any creative project that doesn't fit into other tracks surprise us with bold new ideas.
